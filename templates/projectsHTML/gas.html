Kinetic Theory Simulation

  • Simulated the elastic collisions of up to 2000 particles inside a closed box and tracked their velocity transition through time
  • Performed statistical analysis on the generated data and compared it to the theoretical model of velocity distribution (Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution). The data were 98% compatible with the expected model
  • Used Pandas and NumPy for numerical analysis and Matplotlib to visualize the result
