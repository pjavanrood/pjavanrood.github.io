Algorithmic Trading Bot

  • Coded an Algorithmic trading bot for crypto and stocks that operates in the 1-minute timeframe and returns an average profit of 8% per day. The bot is automated on a VM hosted by the Google cloud
  • Used the Alpaca API and utilized HTTP requests and WebSockets to transfer data
  • Performed quantitative analysis using Pandas and NumPy libraries, and Matplotlib for data visualization
  • Utilized SMA strategy to recognize buy and sell signals, and forecast the most profitable time-period pairs by backtesting
